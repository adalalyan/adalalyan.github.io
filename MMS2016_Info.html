Practical Information


All participants will be accommodated at the Villa Clythia (Address: 2754 Avenue Henri Giraud, 83600 Fréjus).
The conference room and the restaurant (where all lunches and dinners are served) are also in the same building.

Access

  • By railways : TGV to Saint Raphael.
  • By roads : Michelin road maps n° 84 and n° 245. A8 leave Fréjus, head for Cannes (N7 and N98).
  • By plane : Nice (70 km - 45 miles), Toulon (90 km - 55 miles), Marseille (140 km - 85 miles)
 
 
 

Prices
The registration fee is 500 Euros including full board accommodation.
